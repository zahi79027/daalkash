About Daalkash

Welcome to Daalkash, the annual celebration of art, culture, and talent. This festival brings together artists, performers, and enthusiasts from all over Kerala to showcase their skills and compete in a spirit of creativity and camaraderie. Join us for a vibrant experience of tradition and innovation.
